Settings

Corridor each side of route
Range ring on hover / tap
Corridor sets how far either side of your route NM Plotter looks for airfields & frequencies. The range ring is the circle drawn when you hover or tap a field.
Flight recording
Manual records when you tap the record button on the map. Auto starts recording on its own once you're moving (GPS tracking must be on) and stops after you've been stopped a while. Tracks are saved to the Flight Log on this device and export as GPX.
Heads-upiOS pauses background tabs, so recording only runs while NM Plotter is open with the screen on. The screen is kept awake during recording where supported. This is a personal track log, not a certified flight recorder.
